It all started with one small video thanks to which I started to get involved in graphic design, video editing and programming

Like all dudes, I was an ordinary guy who also wanted to get his own computer. And on one of these days my parents gave me a computer. Well, obviously I just played and played on it, but everything changed after I started actively watching YouTube and I suddenly came across a video from Maxim Rusakov, who was just starting his career as a photographer and photo edito I also became interested in video editing. after 1.5 years I decided to meet an illustrator. All the time I honed my skills mainly in Photoshop. All these skills helped me understand what you can think of, create, and realize your fantasies.
2 years after I started studying graphic programs, I was invited to a group of people who were involved in the VKontakte group and the YouTube channel. At that time I was a very happy person. After that, I had the experience of creating memes and comedy videos. After leaving the group, I was actively involved in photoshop, studied and created something new, some of the works were unusual. I was also interested in some paintings, so some of them influenced my creation.
